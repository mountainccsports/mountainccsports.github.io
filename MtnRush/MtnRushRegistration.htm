Mountain Rush Registration

Thank you for registering your child for the Mountain Rush Football Camp.

Here are a few reminders and important things to know about the camp:
  • Dates & Times: Friday, July 29, 6-8:30pm & Saturday, July 30, 9am-1pm
  • Location: Edgewood Recreation Park (Cunion Field), Trimble Rd, Edgewood
  • Check-in: Please arrive at 5:45pm on Friday, July 29th to check in
  • Liability, Medical, Photo Release Form: If you have not already signed and turned in the form, please present a signed form when your child checks in. The form can be accessed and printed HERE.
  • Registration Fee: The $5 camp fee is payable at check-in for those who haven’t paid already.
  • What to bring:
    • A water bottle with your child’s name on it.
    • Players are encouraged to wear football cleats if they have them. We will be on grass.
    • It is likely that sunscreen will be needed on Saturday. Please apply before coming.
    • Your child does NOT need to bring a football. We will have plenty.
  • T-shirts: Camp T-shirts will be given out on Saturday at the conclusion of the camp.

Volunteer: We welcome parent involvement in the Camp. If you would like to volunteer, you may sign up HERE.

It’s going to be a great camp to get your child ready for the upcoming football season. We’re really looking forward to it. If you have any questions, please contact the Camp Director, Luke Erickson, at Luke@MountainCC.org. See you soon!

Luke Erickson
